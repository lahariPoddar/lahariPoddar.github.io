Hi! I am Lahari. Welcome to my homepage. Currently, I work as a Senior Applied Scientist at Microsoft Research. My research here focuses on data driven approaches for foundational model training.

Earlier, I worked as a Senior Applied Scientist in the AGI team at Amazon. I worked towards building Amazon Nova family of foundational models that are capable of solving a wide range of tasks.

Prior to that, I worked in the Customer Service team in Amazon. I developed Generative AI based conversational models that are capable of solving customer issues end-to-end at Amazon scale.

I obtained my PhD degree in Computer Science from National University of Singapore. My thesis focuses on analyzing user generated contents through the lens of opinion mining, and consensus modeling. My research interests include solving real world NLP/IR problems with Probabilistic Graphical Models, and Deep Learning.
